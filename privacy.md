# Privacy Policy — Cause & Cure

**Last updated: 16 July 2026**

## The short version

**Cause & Cure collects nothing about you.** There is no account, no sign-in, no
analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. Nothing you type
into the app ever leaves your device.

## What the app stores, and where

Everything the app knows lives **on your device only**:

- The bundled reference content (diseases, medications, organisms) that ships inside
  the app.
- Anything you add or edit yourself — your own entries, hospital-specific treatment
  notes, review/correction flags, favourites, and quiz progress.
- Your preferences (theme colours, active hospital, disclaimer acknowledgement).

This data is held in the app's private storage on your device. It is not uploaded,
backed up to our servers, or shared with anyone. We have no servers. If you delete
the app, this data is deleted with it.

## Network activity

The app makes exactly one kind of network request: it checks a public file hosted on
GitHub to see whether an updated version of its reference dataset is available, and
downloads it if so.

- This request **sends no information about you** — no identifiers, no usage data,
  no content from your device. It is an ordinary public file download, the same as
  loading a public web page.
- It **uploads nothing, ever.**
- GitHub, as the host of that file, may log the request in the ordinary course of
  serving it (including your IP address), in line with
  [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).
  We neither receive nor have access to those logs.

## Data you must not enter

Cause & Cure is a study reference. **Do not enter patient-identifiable information
into it.** It is not designed, tested, or intended to hold clinical records, and it
offers none of the safeguards such information requires.

## Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone, including
children.

## Medical disclaimer

Cause & Cure is a personal study and reference tool for medical education. It is
**not medical advice** and **not a substitute for professional clinical judgement.**
It must not be used to diagnose, treat, or make decisions for real patients. Drug
doses and treatment information are study drafts curated from third-party references
and may be incomplete, out of date, or incorrect. Always verify against an
authoritative source and current guidelines before any clinical use.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date. The
app collects no data today; were that ever to change, it would be stated here before
it took effect.
